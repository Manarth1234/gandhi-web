Who was Mahatma Gandhi?


Occupation:

An Indian Civil Rights Activist and Politician.

Date of Birth:

2nd October 1869, Porbander, India.

Date of Death:

30th of January 1948, Birla House, New Delhi, India.

Family:

Mohandas Karamchand Gandhi, more known as Mahatma Gandhi was born to Karamchand Gandhi and Putlibai Gandhi. The great hero had two older brothers, Laxmidas Gandhi and Karsandas Gandhi, and an older sister by the name of Raliatbehn. Gandhi married Kasturba Gandhi and had 4 children with her by the name of Harilal Gandhi, Manilal Gandhi, Devdas Gandhi and Ramdas Gandhi.
